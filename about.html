What is PG?

Profit Gaming (“PG”)

PG is a football-gaming ecosystem built around the popular eFootball title. Players register teams, compete in structured online leagues, earn digital rewards and even generate real revenue through ads and daily offers – all managed from their PGL Account.

PG Currency System

  • PG Coins – everyday currency for team fees, post purchases, ad buys, swaps.
  • PG Golds – premium currency; withdrawable & earned from competitions.
  • Manage both inside Telegram via @PGLaccount_bot.

League Structure

1. PG Pro League (Top Tier)

  • 10 teams – home & away, split across two mini-seasons.
  • Bottom 3 relegated ⬇️; 7th enters a playoff.
  • All teams enter the Federation Cup.

2. PG Championship League (Second Tier)

  • 10 teams – single-leg format.
  • Top 2 promoted; 3rd-4th playoff for promotion.
  • Bottom 5 teams are removed from the ladder.
  • Top 3 reach the Federation Cup.

3. PG Upcomings League (Third Tier)

  • Open entry; teams request fixtures via PGL Account.
  • Top 4 promoted to Championship League; no relegation.
  • Top 3 qualify for Federation Cup.

Match & Fair-Play Highlights

  • All matches played in eFootball at the scheduled server time.
  • Screenshot proof required; home team may upload full-match video for bonus coins.
  • Late / no-show penalties: default loss, point deductions & PG Coin fines.
  • Stable connection mandatory – disconnections count as a loss.
  • Cheating, account-sharing or violating KONAMI ToS => permanent ban.

Monetisation Opportunities

  • Daily Reward offers for free coins.
  • Buy Posts: Website & X (Twitter) shout-outs via PG Official accounts.
  • Telegram Ads Marketplace: Team owners can resell cheaper ad slots for profit.
