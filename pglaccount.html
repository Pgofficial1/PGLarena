PGL Account

What is PGL Account?

This is the account manager of each member following PGL. It is where each user can see account balance, register a team, withdraw earnings, buy PG Coins, exchange PG coins with PG golds, and more.

💰 BALANCE

Here you can see your PG Coins and PG Golds balance. Your team name (if registered) is also shown here.

🏆 REGISTER TEAM

You can register your team name for 500 PG Coins. To change the team name later costs 250 PG Coins.

💳 BUY PG COINS

Get information on how to buy PG Coins, used for purchases in the platform.
Note: Include your Telegram ID during purchase.

💸 WITHDRAW

Withdraw your earnings (only in PG Golds) to available payment methods.

🔄 SWAP

Exchange PG Coins ↔ PG Golds as per your preference.

📤 SEND PG COINS

Send PG Coins to other users using their Telegram IDs.

📰 BUY POSTS

Make the platform fun by buying posts for exposure:

  • Website Posts (like magazine news)
  • X Posts (formerly Twitter) to PG Official account

Telegram Ads: Teams can buy ads cheaply and generate income by negotiating with interested advertisers. It's cheaper than official PG ads or Telega.

🎁 DAILY REWARD

Earn free PG Coins through available offers and reward systems.
