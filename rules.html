PG League – Rules & Regulations

These rules govern all competitions held under PG League. All participants must follow the structure, fair play policies, and technical instructions described below.

1. PG PRO LEAGUE (Main League)

  • Consists of 10 teams playing home-and-away matches.
  • The league is divided into 2 seasons – each team plays half of its matches in each season.
  • Points: Win = 3 points, Draw = 1 point.
  • Ranking Criteria:
    • Points → Goal Difference → Head-to-Head.
  • All 10 teams participate in the Federation Cup.
  • Bottom 3 teams are relegated to PG Championship League.
  • 7th place plays a playoff to retain league spot.

2. PG CHAMPIONSHIP LEAGUE (Second Division)

  • Also 10 teams, but matches are single-leg only (no home & away).
  • Top 2 teams are promoted to PG PRO LEAGUE.
  • 3rd and 4th place teams enter promotion playoffs.
  • Bottom 5 teams are terminated (not relegated).
  • Top 3 teams qualify for Federation Cup.

3. PG UPCOMINGS LEAGUE (Third Division)

  • No fixed number of teams. Matches are played upon request (via PGL Account).
  • Ranking is by points, then number of matches played (priority to teams with more matches).
  • If still equal: Goals → Head-to-head.
  • Top 4 teams are promoted to Championship League.
  • No relegation; standings close with PG PRO LEAGUE’s end.
  • Top 3 teams join the Federation Cup.

4. Match Rules

PG PRO & CHAMPIONSHIP LEAGUE:

  • All matches must be played at the specified server time.
  • All players must submit screenshots of match results.
  • The home team can submit full match video or highlights for bonus PG Coins.
  • Home team is responsible for creating match room and sending invitation.

⚠ If an opponent is not present 20 minutes before deadline, report immediately with proof.

⚠ Failure to play without a strong reason results in:

  • Match lost by default (0-3 scoreline).
  • 5-point deduction and 100 PG Coin fine (70 coins awarded to opponent).
  • Suspension from future matches until fine is paid.
  • Missing 3 matches = disqualification from league and relegation/termination.

⚠ Unstable internet = auto lose. Quitting/connection loss penalized.

PG UPCOMINGS LEAGUE:

  • Results must be submitted on time (via screenshot only).
  • Failure to submit screenshot counts as a loss.
  • Matches are played by request; payment required per match.
  • No videos accepted for this league.

5. Awards & Rewards

Prizes for each competition are announced before kickoff. PG Coins, PG Golds, and promotion are common rewards.

6. Fair Play & Cheating

  • Only *eFootball* is allowed as the game format.
  • Any cheating or result manipulation will result in an immediate ban.
  • Do NOT violate KONAMI’s Terms of Service when participating through eFootball. We respect and comply with all official policies.
  • Sharing accounts, match fixing, or using modified APKs will lead to permanent disqualification.
