Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is PG and how does it work?

PG (Profit Gaming) is an online football competition platform using the eFootball game. Players register teams, participate in leagues, earn PG Coins and PG Gold, and can even withdraw real money.

2. What is the difference between PG Coins and PG Gold?

PG Coins are used for in-platform actions like team registration, post ads, etc. PG Gold is the premium currency that can be withdrawn for real money.

3. How do I join a league?

You must register your team in the PGL Account Bot (@PGLaccount_bot) for 500 PG Coins. You will then receive match schedules.

4. How can I earn PG Coins for free?

Earn PG Coins by visiting the Daily Reward page, completing short links, winning matches, and more.

5. How do I buy PG Coins?

Go to "Buy PG Coins" in the PGL Account bot to find instructions. You can pay via PayPal or Airtm and must include your Telegram username.

6. Can I withdraw my PG earnings?

Yes, but only PG Gold is withdrawable. Visit the "Withdraw" section in your PGL Account to request payout.

7. What happens if I miss a scheduled match?

Missing a match without a valid reason will result in a 0-3 loss, 5-point deduction, a fine, and possibly a ban if repeated.

8. Can I change my registered team name?

Yes, renaming costs 250 PG Coins. Use the same team registration function in the bot.

9. How does "Buy Post" work?

You can pay PG Coins to advertise via PG’s website, official X account, or Telegram Ads.

10. How can I send PG Coins to another user?

Use the "Send PG Coins" option in the bot. You’ll need the recipient's Telegram username.

11. Can I advertise my business/brand through PG?

Yes! You can purchase ad posts or Telegram ads. Team owners also have special ad offers for extra income.

12. Who do I contact for help or to report a scam?

Report any issues to PG Officials via @PGOfficals. Never trust unofficial sources.
